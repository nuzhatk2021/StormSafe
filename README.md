🌨️ StormSafe

During extreme weather events, community (stray and feral) animals face immediate survival risks. Residents often encounter animals exposed to dangerous conditions but do not know:

- Whether someone is already helping
- How urgent the situation is
- How to contact volunteers
- What immediate action is appropriate

Current coordination relies on fragmented systems such as social media posts, informal group chats, and direct calls to rescue organizations. These methods lack structure, real-time visibility, and centralized tracking.

StormSafe addresses this gap by providing a structured, location-based emergency coordination system tailored to weather-driven situations.

🎯 Project Goals

-Enable users to report vulnerable animals during extreme weather
-Provide real-time map visibility of reported cases
-Reduce duplicate rescue efforts
-Improve coordination among volunteers and community members
-Surface emergency guidance during official weather alerts

🗺 Core Features (MVP)
📍 Location-based sighting reports
🐾 Interactive map displaying active cases
🚨 Urgency tagging (Shelter Needed, Injured, Food Provided, Resolved)
✅ Status tracking (Open / In Progress / Handled)
🕒 Timestamped reporting
